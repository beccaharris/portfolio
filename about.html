Who am I?

Professionally. I began my career in the field of accounting, but never found it to be the "right" fit. In May of 2016, shortly after I earned my undergraduate degree, I switched career paths and began a job as a quality assurance engineer and almost immediately became fascinated by and, really, fell in love with software development. On my own, I began teaching myself the basics - HTML, CSS, and a few other technologies. To further my knowledge, I enrolled in the University of Denver Coding Bootcamp, which I successfully completed in July of 2018.

Currently, I am still working as a QA engineer for a small software company in Denver Colorado. As my software development skills have grown, I have been able to use them and start developing product features in my current position. My passion for software development continues to grow, and I am eager to continue broadening my knowledge.

Personally. I am a lighthearted and fun-loving person. When I find something I am passionate about, I strive to be the best I can be at it.
